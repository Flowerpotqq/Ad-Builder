# CTA Library

## Primary CTA Patterns by Goal

### Demo Booking
- `Book A Free Demo`
- `See It In Action`
- `Get My Live Walkthrough`

### Trial Activation
- `Start Free Trial`
- `Launch My Workspace`
- `Set Up In 2 Minutes`

### Upgrade/Promotion
- `Activate 20% Savings`
- `Claim My Upgrade Offer`
- `Unlock Pro Features`

### Re-engagement
- `Resume My Account`
- `Pick Up Where I Left Off`
- `Return To Dashboard`

### Transactional Actions
- `Track My Order`
- `Confirm My Email`
- `Reset Password`

## Friction Reducers Near CTA
- `No credit card required`
- `Cancel anytime`
- `Setup support included`
- `Takes less than 3 minutes`

## CTA Placement Rules
1. Hero CTA always present
2. Optional repeated CTA at end for long emails
3. Secondary CTA only for low-risk fallback
