# Deliverability Playbook

## Subject Line Safety
- Keep under 60 characters where possible
- Avoid all-caps or repeated punctuation
- Avoid spam-heavy terms unless context requires it

## Body Safety
- Keep image-heavy layouts balanced with real text
- Match subject promise with body content
- Avoid misleading or hidden terms

## Link Hygiene
- One primary CTA link is preferred
- Keep tracking parameters clean and consistent
- Avoid URL shorteners in core CTA where possible

## Cadence Guidance
- Promotional: no more than 2-3 per week per segment without behavior triggers
- Re-engagement: 2-4 message sequence, then suppress or reduce frequency
- Transactional: event-triggered only

## Content Fidelity
- Exact offer details should match landing page
- Dates, times, and terms must be explicit and consistent
