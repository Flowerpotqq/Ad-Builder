# Subject Line Playbook

## Goals
- Maximize open intent without misleading promises
- Align subject with email type and recipient stage

## Formula Library

### Promotional
- `[Outcome] without [pain]`
- `[Offer] ends [time marker]`
- `A faster way to [desired result]`

Examples:
- `Book more demos this week`
- `Early access pricing ends Friday`
- `Cut follow-up time in half`

### Welcome
- `You are in. Here is your next step`
- `Welcome to {{company_name}}`
- `Start here: your first quick win`

### Newsletter
- `What changed this week in [topic]`
- `3 practical plays for [segment]`
- `Your monthly growth brief`

### Transactional
- `Your order is confirmed`
- `Reset your password`
- `Appointment reminder for [date]`

### Re-engagement
- `Still interested in [outcome]?`
- `We saved your progress`
- `A quick update since your last visit`

## Testing Heuristics
- Test direct value vs curiosity framing
- Test personalization token vs no token
- Test timeline marker vs no timeline marker
- Keep one variable changed per test

## Preview Pairing
The preview text must clarify what the subject implies.
If subject is curiosity-led, preview should be specific.
