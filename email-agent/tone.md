# Tone System

Choose one primary tone per email. Mix only when needed for clarity.

## 1) SaaS Professional

Use for:
- Product launches
- B2B nurture
- Demo booking campaigns

Rules:
- Clear, confident, specific
- Outcome-led language over hype
- Prefer precise claims over adjectives

Word style:
- `automate`, `reduce`, `improve`, `scale`, `visibility`, `control`

Example lines:
- `Route every inbound lead in under 60 seconds.`
- `See campaign performance by channel in one dashboard.`

## 2) Friendly Onboarding

Use for:
- Welcome flows
- First-week activation
- Educational drips

Rules:
- Warm and supportive
- Encourage momentum and small wins
- Avoid technical overload in first touchpoints

Word style:
- `welcome`, `ready`, `start`, `quick win`, `you are set`

Example lines:
- `You are all set, now let us get your first campaign live.`
- `Start with this 2-minute setup and get your first result today.`

## 3) Urgency Promo

Use for:
- Limited-time offers
- Deadline reminders
- Seasonal promotions

Rules:
- Time-aware, not fear-based
- Clear deadline and eligibility
- Keep urgency factual

Word style:
- `ends`, `today`, `window`, `expires`, `last chance`

Example lines:
- `Your early-access pricing ends Friday at midnight.`
- `Claim your onboarding credit before enrollment closes.`

## 4) Conversational Sales

Use for:
- Follow-ups
- Re-engagement nudges
- Founder-led outreach feel

Rules:
- Natural, direct, no hard-sell pressure
- Acknowledge recipient context
- Keep sentences short and human

Word style:
- `quick note`, `wanted to share`, `if helpful`, `pick up where you left off`

Example lines:
- `Quick note, your draft is still waiting and takes 3 minutes to finish.`
- `If timing was the blocker, we can set this up together in one short call.`

## Tone Mixing Rules
- Professional + Urgency: allowed for launch deadlines
- Friendly + Conversational: allowed for onboarding nudges
- Avoid combining all four in one email

## Anti-patterns
- Overclaiming: `guaranteed results`
- Spammy hype: `act now!!!`
- Generic filler: `we hope this email finds you well`
