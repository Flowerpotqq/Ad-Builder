# Quality Checklist

Use this before finalizing any generated email.

## Strategy
- Is one conversion goal clearly prioritized?
- Does email type match recipient state?
- Is chosen framework correctly applied?

## Copy
- Is the headline specific and benefit-led?
- Is copy concise and skimmable?
- Is tone consistent from top to bottom?

## CTA
- Is primary CTA action-specific?
- Is CTA repeated only when useful?
- Are friction reducers included near CTA?

## Design Logic
- Is first screen clear on mobile?
- Are sections visually balanced?
- Is whitespace used intentionally?

## Compliance
- Are terms clear for promotions?
- Is footer present with preference/unsubscribe path?
- Are claims supportable?

## Final Pass
- Remove fluff
- Remove duplicate ideas
- Tighten verbs
- Verify links
